Did you know?

Agriculture employs 1.34 billion people globally. Smallholder farmers produce and sell nearly 70 per cent of the food consumed worldwide but remain financially excluded and increasingly vulnerable to changing climate patterns affecting their yields.

Technology has played a big role in developing the agricultural industry. Today it is possible to grow crops in a desert by use of agricultural biotechnology. With this technology, plants have been engineered to survive in drought conditions. .

Through genetic engineering scientists have managed to introduce traits into existing genes with a goal of making crops resistant to droughts and pests
